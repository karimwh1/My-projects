Conclusions¶

  1. Females are more likely to miss the appointment
  2. Upon recieving an SMS the patient is more likely to attend the appointment on time.
  3. The less waiting time the more likely that the patient will not miss the appoinment.
  4. People who have experience with chronic disease are more likely to not miss the appoinment.

Limitations¶

  1. There were spelling mistakes which would hinder the analysis process.
  2. Some values were needed to be changed for a smoother operation.
  3. I could not exactly know why the patients did not attend but I could find factors that could increase the likelyhood of the patients not missing (such as the SMS).
  4. I could not find a specific reason why a certain area has more patients missing their appointments contrary to other areas.
